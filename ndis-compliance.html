NDIS Compliance Statement.

Our commitment to safe, respectful and accountable support.

Commitment

Aussie Wide Disability and Health Services Pty Ltd is committed to delivering services in accordance with the NDIS Practice Standards and Code of Conduct.

We ensure

  • Respect for participant rights and dignity
  • Safe and competent service delivery
  • Staff screening and qualification verification
  • Incident management procedures
  • Complaints handling processes
  • Ongoing quality improvement

Choice and inclusion

We promote participant choice, control and inclusion in all service planning and delivery.

Professional disability and healthcare services delivered with clinical expertise, respect and compassion.
